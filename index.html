About me

Hello! My name is Ni'Gere Epps. I am currently completing my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction at The University of Chicago. I am also minoring in Media Arts & Design and will be graduating in June 2021.

I have worked in numerous fields and interned at a wide range of companies. This has given me experience and skills in IT, software developement, data management, SEO, marketing, sales, community engagement, HR, and product management.
